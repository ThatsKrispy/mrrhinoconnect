Network Requirements for IPTV

IPTV is more demanding on your network than standard internet browsing — each HD stream requires 8–15 Mbps of reliable, low-latency bandwidth with multicast support on your managed switches. MrRhinoConnect assesses your existing network infrastructure as part of every IPTV project scope and includes any necessary network upgrades in the proposal.

Regulatory & Rights Compliance

Commercial IPTV requires the appropriate FCC-registered content licenses (Public Performance License) for common-area displays. MrRhinoConnect ensures your system is configured correctly and your content licenses are appropriate for each type of viewing location on your property.
