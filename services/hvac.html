Energy Efficiency in the Florida Heat

HVAC typically represents 40–60% of a commercial building's energy bill in South Florida. Modern high-efficiency equipment with SEER ratings of 18–22 can reduce cooling costs by 30–40% vs. legacy systems. Combined with smart controls and occupancy scheduling, the payback on a system replacement is often under 5 years.
