Commercial Solar Installation Across Miami-Dade, Broward & Palm Beach

Florida's solar market has grown dramatically — and the economics have never been better for South Florida commercial properties. The combination of 8–11¢/kWh FPL commercial electricity rates, 2,800+ annual sun hours, the 30% federal Investment Tax Credit, Florida's complete property tax exemption on solar improvements, and sales tax exemption on solar equipment makes commercial solar one of the most compelling capital investments available to property owners.

Federal & State Incentives

The Inflation Reduction Act's 30% Investment Tax Credit (ITC) applies to commercial solar installations placed in service through 2032. Florida properties also benefit from FS 193.624's 100% property tax exemption on the added value from solar — meaning your property assessment doesn't increase when you add solar. Combined with FPL's net metering program, the financial picture is compelling.

System Monitoring & Maintenance

MrRhinoConnect's solar systems include real-time production monitoring platforms (SolarEdge, Enphase, or SMA) that show generation data, system health, and estimated savings. We offer annual inspection and cleaning contracts to ensure your system maintains its warranted output over its 25+ year life.
