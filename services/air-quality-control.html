Indoor Air Quality Solutions for South Florida Properties

South Florida's humid subtropical climate creates unique IAQ challenges — high ambient humidity promotes mold growth in HVAC ductwork and building cavities, while constant air conditioning recirculates stale air for months on end. MrRhinoConnect's IAQ systems are specifically configured for these conditions, with humidity-aware sensor thresholds and coil treatments that address mold at the source.

WELL Building Standard & LEED IAQ Credits

IAQ monitoring and filtration upgrades can contribute credits toward WELL Building Standard certification and LEED v4 Indoor Environmental Quality categories. We provide documentation for all installed systems and can work with your LEED consultant to identify which measures qualify for your certification path.

South Florida Mold & Humidity Considerations

South Florida HVAC systems run near 100% of the year, making coil fouling and drain pan contamination constant concerns. Our air quality maintenance contracts include quarterly HVAC coil inspection, drain pan treatments, and filter replacements — keeping both air quality and equipment efficiency high.
