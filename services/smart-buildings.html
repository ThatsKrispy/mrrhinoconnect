Your Building Is Smarter Than You Think — We Connect the Dots

Modern South Florida buildings are full of technology — HVAC systems, access control platforms, security cameras, lighting controls, EV chargers, and IoT sensors — but most of it operates in isolated silos. MrRhinoConnect's smart building integration practice connects these disparate systems into a unified building intelligence platform that reduces operating costs, improves tenant experience, and gives property managers real-time control from a single dashboard.

We work with all major building automation platforms — Honeywell Forge, Siemens Desigo, Johnson Controls OpenBlue, and open-standard BACnet/Modbus systems — and can integrate legacy equipment that existing vendors claim can't talk to modern platforms.
