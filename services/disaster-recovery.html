Disaster Recovery Built for South Florida's Reality

Most DR plans are written by vendors who've never experienced a Category 4 hurricane or a ransomware incident that encrypts an entire school district's file server. MrRhinoConnect has. We design recovery programs that account for the conditions your organization actually faces.

Know Your Numbers: RTO and RPO

Before we design anything, we identify your real tolerance for downtime and data loss. How many hours can you operate without email? How much transactional data can you afford to lose? Every architecture decision flows from these numbers.

Immutable Cloud Backup

Ransomware specifically targets backup systems. We deploy air-gapped, immutable cloud backups that cannot be encrypted, deleted, or modified — even by someone with admin credentials. Backups replicate automatically, retention policies are enforced, and restoration is tested on a schedule you approve.

Connectivity Redundancy

South Florida's infrastructure gets tested every storm season. Our SD-WAN solutions bond multiple ISP connections — fiber, coax, cellular — and fail over automatically in under 60 seconds when a primary circuit goes down. Critical applications stay running, users notice nothing.

Documented, Tested Runbooks

A DR plan that exists only in someone's head is not a plan. We document every recovery procedure — step by step, role by role — and test them on a scheduled basis. Your team can execute a recovery with or without your IT lead in the room.
