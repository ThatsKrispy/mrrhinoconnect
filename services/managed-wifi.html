Our managed WiFi services ensure that every corner of your property enjoys seamless and high-speed internet connectivity. Unlike consumer-grade setups, our solutions include centralized management, QoS traffic prioritization, guest network isolation, and proactive monitoring.

What Is Managed WiFi?

Managed WiFi is a fully deployed and maintained wireless network where MrRhinoConnect handles everything — from initial site survey and equipment installation to ongoing monitoring, troubleshooting, and firmware updates. Property managers get one point of contact for all connectivity needs.

Key Benefits

Full Property Coverage

Professional site surveys ensure access points are placed for complete coverage — no dead zones in lobbies, parking garages, pools, or hallways.

Proactive Monitoring

Our team monitors network performance 24/7, identifying and resolving issues before tenants experience disruptions.

Guest & Tenant Segmentation

Separate guest networks, tenant networks, and management networks for security and performance isolation.
