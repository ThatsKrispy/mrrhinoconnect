Modern Light. Lower Bills. FPL Rebates.

Lighting represents 20–30% of a typical commercial building's energy consumption in South Florida — and most properties still have fluorescent or HID fixtures that waste significant energy and require frequent costly lamp replacements. MrRhinoConnect's LED retrofit program replaces outdated lighting with high-efficiency LEDs, delivering immediate energy savings, improved light quality, and FPL rebate qualification.

We handle complete end-to-end LED projects — photometric layouts to ensure proper foot-candle levels, permit procurement, licensed installation, FPL rebate documentation, and disposal of old fixtures through approved channels. Most commercial LED retrofits pay back in 18–36 months through energy savings alone.

Commercial LED Retrofit Across Miami-Dade, Broward & Palm Beach

South Florida's year-round sunshine and high electricity rates make LED lighting upgrades uniquely compelling. FPL's average commercial rate of 9–11 cents per kWh, combined with 8,760 hours of annual lighting hours in 24/7 parking and common areas, means the energy savings from LED can be substantial — often $15,000–$100,000+ per year for larger properties.

FPL Commercial Rebate Programs

Florida Power & Light offers the Commercial & Industrial Efficiency Program that provides prescriptive rebates for LED lamp and fixture replacements. MrRhinoConnect handles the rebate application process — identifying qualifying fixtures, calculating savings, and submitting documentation on your behalf. We've helped South Florida clients recover $10,000–$80,000 in FPL rebates on LED projects.

Lighting Design & Photometric Analysis

We don't just swap old bulbs for new ones. Our LED projects begin with a photometric analysis using Dialux or AGi32 software to ensure the replacement fixtures deliver the correct foot-candle levels for each space type — IES standards for parking, OSHA minimums for staircases, and ASHRAE 90.1 compliance for permitted renovation projects.
