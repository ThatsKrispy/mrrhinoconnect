Distributed Antenna System (DAS)

Enhanced emergency responder radio communication in buildings

Our Services

Ensure Emergency Responders Stay Connected in Your Building

Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) ensure that cellular and emergency responder radio signals penetrate throughout your building — including basements, stairwells, parking garages, and other signal-challenged areas. Many South Florida municipalities now require DAS systems in buildings exceeding certain size thresholds.

Why DAS Matters

Emergency Responder Compliance

Meet NFPA 72 and local fire code requirements for in-building public safety radio coverage — mandatory in many commercial buildings.

Cellular Signal Amplification

Boost cellular signals from all major carriers throughout your building, eliminating dead zones in basements and parking structures.

Testing & Certification

Our DAS installations include required testing, documentation, and certification for fire marshal approval and occupancy permits.
