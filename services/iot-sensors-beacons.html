The Intelligence Layer of Your Smart Building

A smart building is only as smart as the data it can see. MrRhinoConnect designs and deploys IoT sensor networks that give South Florida property managers real-time insight into occupancy, energy use, environmental conditions, equipment health, and security events — all feeding into a unified dashboard that drives better decisions and lower operating costs.

Our sensor deployments range from simple occupancy-based lighting control to complex multi-protocol networks that tie HVAC, lighting, access control, energy meters, and environmental sensors into a single building intelligence platform.

Occupancy Sensors

PIR and computer vision sensors that detect room occupancy for HVAC and lighting control — reducing waste in empty spaces.

Environmental Monitors

Temperature, humidity, CO2, VOC, and air pressure sensors that feed HVAC and ventilation control systems.

Bluetooth Beacons

BLE beacons for indoor wayfinding, visitor analytics, asset tracking, and proximity marketing in hospitality and retail.

Equipment Health Sensors

Vibration, temperature, and current sensors on HVAC, elevators, and pumps for predictive maintenance alerts.

IoT Sensor Networks for South Florida Smart Buildings

The South Florida commercial real estate market is increasingly demanding smart building certifications — WiredScore, WELL, LEED — that require demonstrable technology infrastructure. IoT sensor networks are foundational to these certifications and provide the data streams that building automation systems, energy managers, and sustainability reports depend on.

Protocol & Platform Compatibility

We deploy sensors across multiple protocols — Zigbee, Z-Wave, BACnet, Modbus, MQTT, and LoRaWAN — and integrate them into platforms like Honeywell Forge, Siemens Desigo, Johnson Controls OpenBlue, or open-source platforms like Home Assistant for smaller properties. We spec the right protocol for your existing infrastructure.

Data Security & Privacy

IoT networks are a growing attack surface. MrRhinoConnect segments sensor networks on dedicated VLANs, encrypts data in transit, implements certificate-based device authentication, and provides quarterly security audits to keep your building's data infrastructure protected.
