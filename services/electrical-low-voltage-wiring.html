Licensed Low-Voltage Electrical for Every Building System

Low-voltage wiring is the nervous system of a modern building — it connects access control readers to controllers, intercom panels to door strikes, fire alarm initiating devices to control panels, and AV systems to amplifiers and displays. MrRhinoConnect's licensed low-voltage electricians install, terminate, and commission all of these systems across South Florida.

We pull the wire, terminate the devices, program the controllers, and test every circuit before we leave. If a system fails inspection, we fix it at no extra charge.

Access Control Wiring

Door readers, REX sensors, door strikes, and magnetic locks wired to controllers and tied into your access platform.

Intercom & Entry Systems

Multi-tenant intercom systems, video entry panels, and visitor management systems for residential and commercial properties.

Fire Alarm Low Voltage

Notification appliance circuits (NAC), initiating device circuits (IDC), and signaling line circuits (SLC) for fire alarm panels.

AV & PA System Wiring

Speaker cables, video distribution, control wiring, and rack terminations for AV and public address systems.

South Florida Experts

Licensed Low-Voltage Wiring Throughout Miami-Dade, Broward & Palm Beach

Florida requires a state-issued Electrical Contractor license (EC) or Low Voltage license (LV) to legally install most low-voltage systems in commercial buildings. MrRhinoConnect's team holds the appropriate licenses for all systems we install — so your building passes inspection and your liability is covered.
