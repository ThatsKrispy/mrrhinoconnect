Networking & Connectivity

The Backbone Behind Every Modern Building System

Every smart building, WiFi network, security camera system, and IPTV deployment runs on physical cable. MrRhinoConnect's commercial cabling crews install, terminate, and certify structured cabling systems for South Florida properties of every size — from a 10-unit boutique condo in Coral Gables to a 500,000 sq ft office campus in Doral.

We install Cat6A twisted-pair for data and VoIP, single-mode and multi-mode fiber for backbone runs, RG-6 quad-shield coax for IPTV and cable TV distribution, and low-voltage conduit for future-proof infrastructure. Every run is tested and certified with a Fluke DSX CableAnalyzer.

Certified Structured Cabling Across South Florida

South Florida's commercial cabling market has a quality problem — too many cut-rate shops that skip certification testing, leave unterminated cables, and disappear when something fails. MrRhinoConnect holds BICSI and manufacturer certifications and backs every installation with a 25-year channel warranty on certified cabling systems.

What Cabling Certification Means

We test every cable run with a Fluke DSX-600 CableAnalyzer and provide printouts for every link — verifying return loss, insertion loss, NEXT, and alien crosstalk to TIA-568.2-D Category 6A standards. That certification is what qualifies your cabling for the 25-year manufacturer warranty and proves to tenants and future buyers that the infrastructure is solid.
